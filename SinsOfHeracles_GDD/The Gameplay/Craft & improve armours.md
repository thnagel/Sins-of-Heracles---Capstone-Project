## **Armor Crafting & Upgrading**

### **Armor Crafting**
Armor can be crafted at **any blacksmith** the player encounters.

- Requires a **specific set of materials** depending on the armor type  
- Materials are **primarily obtained by defeating enemies**  
- Higher-tier armor demand **rarer drops** and larger quantities  
- Encourages exploration and combat to gather resources  

---

### **Armor Improvement**
Players can **upgrade their existing armor sets**.

- Upgrades increase **defense values**, **elemental resistances**, or **special properties**  
- Armor upgrades are performed at **any blacksmith**  
- Each upgrade requires:  
  - **Base materials** from defeated enemies  
  - **Rare enhancement items** found through exploration or boss encounters  
- Upgrade tiers are limited to maintain balance and encourage meaningful choices  

---

### **Material Acquisition**
Materials are dropped by enemies, with rarity tied to enemy type and difficulty:

- **Common enemies** drop standard crafting materials  
- **Elite enemies** drop advanced or rare materials  
- **Bosses** drop unique items required for high-tier upgrades  

---

### **Crafting Philosophy**
The crafting loop is designed to:

- Reward consistent combat and exploration  
- Provide clear progression through equipment upgrades  
- Allow players to personalize weapons and armor  
- Keep upgrades meaningful without overwhelming the player with complex systems
