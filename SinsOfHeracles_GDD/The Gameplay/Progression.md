## **Player Progression** 

Experience System: Souls-like progression, enemies drop “Ichor” used to level up stats. 

Stats: Strength, Dexterity, Vitality, Endurance, Faith(god powers?). 

Gear Upgrades: Weapons and armor can be upgraded using materials gathered in each level. 

Skill Unlocks: Minor passive boosts (e.g., faster stamina recovery, increased healing effectiveness).
