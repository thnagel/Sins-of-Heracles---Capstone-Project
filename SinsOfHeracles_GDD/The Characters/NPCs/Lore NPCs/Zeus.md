## In the myths :

In Greek mythology, Zeus is the king of the gods, god of thunder and the sky, lord of [Olympus](obsidian://open?vault=SinsOfHercales_GDD&file=The%20World%2FLore%20places%2FMount%20Olympus), the residence of the gods, and husband of [Hera](obsidian://open?vault=SinsOfHercales_GDD&file=The%20Characters%2FNPCs%2FLore%20NPCs%2FHera), queen of the gods and goddess of marriage and fertility.
He is also the biological father of [Heracles](obsidian://open?vault=SinsOfHercales_GDD&file=The%20Characters%2FHeracles). One night, Zeus took advantage of the absence of Amphitryon, king of Thebes, to seduce his wife, Alcmene. The king of the gods committed another act of infidelity by transforming himself into Amphitryon. From this affair, Heracles was born.
## In *Sins of Heracles* :

In [*Sins of Heracles*](obsidian://open?vault=SinsOfHercales_GDD&file=Sins%20of%20Heracles), Zeus does not appear and no dialogue or interaction with him is possible.
