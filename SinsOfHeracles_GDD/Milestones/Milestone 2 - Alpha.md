## Alpha (Playable Slice with Hub + First Labor)

**Goal:** Create the first fully playable vertical slice of the game, connecting the hub world (Tiryns) and the first level (The Nemean Lion). 

 Deliverables: 

> Hub Area: City of Tiryns: 
- Basic layout and navigable city environment. 
- Functional vendors (UI placeholders). 
- Dialogue/interaction system for King Eurystheus (quest giver). 

> 1st Labor: The Nemean Lion: 
- Complete level flow (exploration + boss). 
- Unique enemies, traps, and shortcuts. 
- Boss fight with full mechanics and animations. 

> Progression Systems: 
- Level-up system using “Ichor”. 
- Basic weapon/armor upgrade system. 

> Save/Load Functionality: 
- Shrines of Olympus working as rest/checkpoints. 

> UI & Feedback: 
- Combat HUD, inventory menu, simple pause menu. 

> Sound & Music (Temp): 
- Placeholder sound effects and early ambient music. 

**Purpose:** Deliver a fully playable alpha version demonstrating the core gameplay, world tone, and progression loop. This version should be internally playable from starting in Tiryns → completing the Nemean Lion → returning to Tiryns.
