## Beta (Content-Complete Version)

**Goal:** Finalize all planned content (2 labors + hub) and polish for stability, performance, and player experience. 

 Deliverables: 

> 2nd Labor: The Lernaean Hydra: 
- Full level with enemies, hazards, and boss fight. 
- Environmental storytelling and lore items integrated. 

> Narrative Integration: 
- Voice lines or cutscenes for King Eurystheus and major characters. 
- Updated hub to reflect progression after each labor. 

> Art & Audio Pass: 
- Final or near-final visuals for environments, characters, and UI. 
- Polished sound effects and adaptive soundtrack. 

> Optimization & QA: 
- Bug fixing, performance profiling, controller tuning. 
- Early playtesting and balance feedback. 

> Packaging: 
- Intro cinematic or title screen. 
- Save/load stability. 
- Build ready for external testing or presentation. 

**Purpose:** Deliver a content-complete and polished build representing the full intended scope for the first release phase of Sins of Heracles.
