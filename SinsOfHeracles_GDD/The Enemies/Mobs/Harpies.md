## In the myth :

Harpies are mythological creatures, goddesses of devastation and divine vengeance described as beasts with bird bodies and women's heads. Harpies appear in Homer's *Odyssey*, Virgil's *Aeneid*, and the myth of Jason and the Golden Fleece.

## In *Sins of Heracles* :

In [*Sins of Heracles*](obsidian://open?vault=SinsOfHercales_GDD&file=Sins%20of%20Heracles), harpies are recurring monsters that can be found in the [Nemean plains](obsidian://open?vault=SinsOfHeracles_GDD&file=The%20World%2FMain%20places%2FNemean%20plains).
